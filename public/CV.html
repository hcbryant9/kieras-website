Kiera O'Harrow

Kiera O’Harrow is an artist, designer, and magpie working mainly with physical objects and photography. Her work has been shown in the Anderson Gallery, Black Iris Gallery, Gallery 405, VCUQatar, VCUQatar house (Richmond), Rag N Bones Bike Shop, as well as several pop ups. Utilizing metal sculpture, photography, found objects, and mixed media, her practice explores interconnection in all the ways it transcends individuality and brings us closer to each other and the non-human worlds around us. O’Harrow has received several awards from VCUarts, most recently including two research grants to explore Scandinavian design and create a photobook about FOMO and social media. Outside of her sculptural practice, O’Harrow has worked in fabrication and outdoor technical education/facilitation.
